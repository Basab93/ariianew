About Us

Who we are and what drives ARIIA 2026

Our Story

ARIIA is a collaborative initiative to advance Augmented Reality, Intelligent Systems, and Industrial Automation. The 2026 edition unites academia, industry, and policy makers to explore breakthroughs, share applications, and accelerate the adoption of next-generation smart systems.

Hosted by Dayananda Sagar University with MIT (MAHE) and Manipal University Jaipur as partners, and in association with the University of Warwick and the University of Malta, ARIIA 2026 emphasizes impactful research, ethical innovation, and global collaboration.
