Paper Submission

Original, unpublished research only • Blind review • Hybrid conference

Submission Guidelines

Academicians, researchers and industry practitioners are invited to submit their original research articles, previously unpublished and not currently under review by another conference or journal.

Critical Information
  • Only Original Research Articles are accepted.
  • Review Articles are not accepted.
  • All submissions undergo rigorous peer review.
  • Acceptance based on quality, relevance & originality.
  • At least one author must register & present.
Instructions for Submission
  • Full paper ≤ 8 pages (including references).
  • Use the official template (below).
  • Similarity index ≤ 15%.
  • Similarity index ≤ 20%.
  • Upload as PDF only.
  • Don’t combine the paper & similarity report.
