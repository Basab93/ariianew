ARIIA 2026

Explore the Research Tracks

ARIIA 2026 invites original contributions aligned with the following research tracks. Topics include, but are not limited to:

Robotics and Industrial Automation

  • Control and Automation
  • 3D Printing & CAD Modelling
  • Humanoid & Soft Robotics
  • Machine Vision & Perception
  • Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)
  • Healthcare Automation

Industry 4.0 and 5.0

  • Digital Twin & Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Augmented Reality & Human–Machine Collaboration
  • Predictive Maintenance
  • Green & Smart Manufacturing
  • IIoT and Data Analytics
  • Automation in Agriculture

Bioengineering and Medical Technologies

  • Biomedical Sensors & Wearables
  • Health Informatics & AR/VR Applications
  • Biomedical Imaging & Diagnostics
  • Therapeutic & Assistive Robotics
  • Nanomaterials in Medicine

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

  • Deep & Reinforcement Learning
  • Generative AI & Computer Vision
  • AI for Healthcare & Agriculture
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Data Mining & Big Data Analytics

Communication and Networking

  • 5G & Beyond Wireless Networks
  • IoT and Edge Computing
  • Blockchain in Communication
  • Cybersecurity and Privacy
  • Optical and Cognitive Networks

If your paper spans multiple domains, please select the closest primary track during submission. The program chairs may reassign papers for optimal review.

ARIIA

The International Conference on Augmented Reality, Intelligent Systems, and Industrial Automation (ARIIA-2026) is organised by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (AI & ML), Dayananda Sagar University. The conference promotes exchange of ideas, research, and industry collaboration.
